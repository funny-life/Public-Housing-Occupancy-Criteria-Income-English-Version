Special Exemption The applicant or a relative living together with the applicant who does not fall under the category of "single parent", has a total income of 5,000,000 yen or less, has no one who is deemed to be in a de facto marriage-like situation, and falls under any of the following categories Those who are divorced from their husbands and are not married and have dependents. Those who have not been married since the death of their husbands or whose husbands' deaths are not known.

Special Exemption The applicant or a relative living together who is not currently married or whose spouse's life or death is not known, and who meets all three of the following requirements: a, b, and c. a,There is no one who is deemed to be in a situation similar to that of a de facto marriage. b,You must have a child (whose gross income for the year is 480,000 yen or less and who is not the same living spouse or dependent of another person) who makes a living together. c,Total income must be less than 5,000,000 yen.
